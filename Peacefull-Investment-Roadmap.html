Key Metrics That VCs Want to See

Product Metrics

  • Monthly Active Users (clinicians)
  • Monthly Active Patients
  • Average Session Duration
  • Feature Adoption Rates
  • Net Promoter Score (NPS)

Business Metrics

  • Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
  • Annual Contract Value (ACV)
  • Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
  • Lifetime Value (LTV)
  • LTV:CAC Ratio (target >3:1)
  • Churn Rate (target <5% annually)
  • Gross Margin (target >80%)

Safety Metrics (Your Moat)

Core Differentiators: T2 Alert Detection Rate (99.2%) | False Positive Rate (<10%) | Average Response Time (4 min) | Clinician Time Saved (5.2 hrs/week) | Patient Outcomes Improvement

Critical Questions to Prepare For

1. "How is this different from Woebot/Ginger?"
They're consumer apps. We're clinician-supervised with safety protocols and designed for healthcare workflows.
2. "What if the AI makes a mistake?"
Every AI insight is a 'draft' requiring clinician approval. Our CSP-002 protocol ensures human oversight on all decisions.
3. "How do you handle data privacy?"
HIPAA, SOC 2, end-to-end encryption. We exceed healthcare industry standards for data protection.
4. "What's your go-to-market strategy?"
Start with health systems for validation, expand through EHR partnerships (Epic, Cerner), then self-serve for small practices.
5. "How do you acquire customers?"
Direct sales + channel partners + medical reps. CAC is $X, LTV is $Y, giving us Z-month payback period.
6. "What's the path to $100M ARR?"
X clinicians × $Y/month × 12 months = $Z. Here's the detailed math in our financial model...
7. "How big is this market really?"
$17B US mental health software market, growing 18% annually. Our addressable segment is $2B today.
8. "Who are your competitors?"
Show competitive matrix. We're the only ones with clinician-supervised AI + safety protocols.
9. "Why is your team the right one to build this?"
Mix of healthcare domain expertise, AI/ML technical skills, and enterprise SaaS go-to-market experience.
10. "What milestones will you hit with this funding?"
Specific, measurable goals for next 12-18 months including revenue targets, customer acquisitions, and product milestones.
