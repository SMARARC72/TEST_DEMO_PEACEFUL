This 8-10 minute workflow simulation demonstrates the core value proposition: clinician-supervised AI that captures between-session signals, generates draft summaries, and provides transparent decision support.

Patient Journaling & Voice

              ~1 min
            

            
Patient submits journal entry and async voice note. System processes with ASR and safety detection.

Clinician Portal Inbox (T2 Alert)

              ~1 min
            

            
Safety signal detected → Portal inbox item created (authoritative) → Email/SMS nudges sent.

Inbox Detail + Evidence Panel

              ~1 min
            

            
Clinician reviews alert with segment-level evidence references. Acknowledges or resolves in portal only.

            
          

        

        
        

          
4

          

            

              
Draft Summary Review

              ~1 min

5

          

            

              
Suppressed Recommendation

              ~1 min

Exports Center + Manifest

              ~1 min

ROI Assumptions Panel

              ~1 min
            

            
Pilot goals with assumptions drawer. Toggle between pilot targets and projections with formula transparency.
