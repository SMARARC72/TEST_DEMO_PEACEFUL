# Go-to-Market Strategy
## Behavioral Health AI Companion

**Date:** 2026-02-25  
**Planning Horizon:** 24 Months  
**Target Market:** US Healthcare Systems

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Our go-to-market strategy is designed to achieve **$2M ARR within 12 months** and **$10M ARR within 24 months** through a three-phase approach:

1. **Direct Sales** (Months 1-12): Land 50 health systems through enterprise sales
2. **EHR Partnerships** (Months 12-24): Scale through Epic/Cerner distribution
3. **Self-Serve** (Months 24+): Expand to private practices

**Key Metrics:**
- CAC: $4,500
- ACV: $18,000
- LTV:CAC Ratio: 4:1
- Sales Cycle: 3 months (pilot to contract)
- Pilot Conversion Rate: 67%

**Size:** 50-500 mental health clinicians

4. VP of Population Health

## VALUE PROPOSITION BY SEGMENT

### Health Systems
> "Reduce clinician documentation time by 5.2 hours per week while improving patient safety with 99.2% risk detection accuracy. Our AI-augmented platform helps you catch crises 60x faster and support 3.2x more patients per clinician."

**ROI Calculator:**
- 100 clinicians × 5.2 hours saved × $60/hour = $31,200/week
- 100 clinicians × $150/month = $180,000/year platform cost
- **Net Savings: $1.4M annually**

### Private Practices
> "Spend more time with patients and less time on documentation. Our AI helps you monitor patients between sessions, automatically generate session notes, and never miss a concerning pattern."

**ROI Calculator:**
- 5 clinicians × 5.2 hours saved × $100/hour = $2,600/week
- 5 clinicians × $50/month = $3,000/year platform cost
- **Net Savings: $132,000 annually**
