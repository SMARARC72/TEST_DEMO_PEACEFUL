## CONCLUSION

This roadmap balances **ambition with execution**. We're building the foundation in Q1, scaling through integrations in Q2-Q3, and expanding to become the platform of choice for AI-augmented mental health care by 2027.

**Key Principles:**
1. Safety first — never compromise on patient safety
2. Clinician trust — every feature must earn clinician confidence
3. Measurable outcomes — everything we build must show ROI
4. Regulatory compliance — build for healthcare from day one
