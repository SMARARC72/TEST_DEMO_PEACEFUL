# Behavioral Health AI Companion
## Executive Summary

---

### THE COMPANY
**Behavioral Health AI Companion** is a clinician-supervised AI platform that provides 24/7 mental health monitoring, automated documentation, and real-time crisis detection. We bridge the critical gap between therapy sessions, helping clinicians detect concerning patterns 60x faster while saving 5.2 hours per week on documentation.

**Founded:** 2025 | **Stage:** Seed | **Raising:** $10M

---

### THE PROBLEM

Mental health care is in crisis:
- **68%** of mental health crises occur between therapy sessions
- **30%** of concerning patterns go unnoticed by clinicians
- **4+ hours** average response time to patient crises
- **$50B** annual cost of untreated mental health conditions in the US

Clinicians are burned out, patients fall through the cracks, and health systems struggle to provide continuous care.

---

### THE SOLUTION

Our AI-powered platform provides:

| Feature | Impact |
|---------|--------|
| **24/7 Patient Monitoring** | Continuous analysis of journal entries, check-ins, voice notes |
| **Real-Time Risk Detection** | 99.2% accuracy in identifying T2/T1/T0 alerts |
| **Automated Documentation** | AI-generated summaries save 5.2 hours/week per clinician |
| **Clinician-Supervised AI** | Every insight requires clinician approval (CSP-002) |

**The Result:** 4-hour response time → 4 minutes (60x faster)
