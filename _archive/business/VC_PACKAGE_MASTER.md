## 🎯 INVESTMENT THESIS

### The Opportunity
**Behavioral Health AI Companion** is a clinician-supervised AI platform that provides 24/7 mental health monitoring, automated documentation, and real-time crisis detection.

### The Problem
- 68% of mental health crises occur between therapy sessions
- 30% of concerning patterns go unnoticed
- 4+ hours average response time
- $50B annual cost of untreated mental health

### The Solution
- 24/7 patient monitoring with AI analysis
- 99.2% T2 alert detection accuracy
- 5.2 hours saved per clinician weekly
- 60x faster crisis response (4 hours → 4 minutes)

### The Market
- **TAM:** $17B US Mental Health Software
- **SAM:** $2B AI-Assisted Mental Health
- **SOM:** $100M (3-year target)

### The Business
- **Model:** SaaS ($150/clinician/month)
- **Unit Economics:** LTV $18K, CAC $4.5K, 4:1 ratio
- **Traction:** 3 pilots, 45 clinicians, $85K ARR
- **Ask:** $10M Seed for $2M ARR in 12 months
